## Content Tips

### For Project Descriptions
- **What**: Clear 1-2 sentence project description
- **Why**: Problem it solved or gap it filled
- **How**: Technologies & approach used
- **Impact**: Results, metrics, or achievements

### For Skills Section
- **Organize logically** by category/tier
- **Show progression** (Expert → Advanced → Intermediate)
- **Include both technical and soft skills**
- **Make scannable** with badges or tags

### For Timeline
- **Reverse chronological order** (newest first)
- **Include location** for context
- **Highlight key achievements** per role
- **Show career progression** & growth
